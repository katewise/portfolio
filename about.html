About

Hi, I'm Kate – a UX/UI Designer who's always on the lookout for new and exciting ways to express my creativity.
I've got a keen eye for photography, love putting together visually stunning videos, and enjoy flexing my artistic muscles through illustration.
For me, it's all about the journey – solving problems, learning new skills, and exploring fresh ideas. The end result is just the icing on the cake!

↓

For over a decade, I honed my culinary skills as a chef and baker, owning my own café and serving my country as a chef in the navy. As much as I loved cooking, I knew there was more to my creative abilities. That's when I embarked on a new career path in design, I'm thrilled to put my skills to use and bring fresh, innovative ideas to life.

I've filled countless visual diaries with sketches, scribbles, and wild ideas, fueled by endless cups of tea and the entire Taylor Swift discography on repeat (multiple times, I might add).

When it comes down to it, art is an integral part of who I am. That's why I'm so excited to be on this new career path, where I can channel my creativity into exciting new projects and explore new forms of artistic expression.
With every passing day, I'm discovering new ways to push my limits and create unique and innovative work. I can't wait to see what we can create together in the future!
