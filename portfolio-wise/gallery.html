Flours. - A baking blog

The process for this blog included everything from field reviews of other popular baking blogs, personas, user journeys, empathy mapping, research on blog layouts, brand design, logo creation, user testing, a mobile and desktop friendly final website, baking A LOT, and all the photography.

Sale and swap market event for Seconds


I designed the Seconds sale and swap market to help reduce the impact of fast fashion and create a place for like-minded individuals to sell or swap their unwanted clothing.


The logo was created to give a collage effect, bringing together different people where no piece of clothing would be the same, I wanted all the branding to reflect this scrapbook effect.


The process included -Brand design, logo creation,photography, wireframes, XD prototype, user testing, persona creation, user journeys, empathy maps, field reviews, SWOT analysis of competitors.

Motion Design

During my second year of university, I discovered a passion for videography and motion. Motivated by this newfound interest, I embarked on a creative project that revolved around capturing the art I made on film. Over the course of eight weeks, I worked on creating something new every day - be it a painting, sketch, clay model, or any other mixed media. To ensure every project was captured for documentation purposes I filmed each one. In the end, I finished the project with a cinematic short film named Acrylic on Canvas.
