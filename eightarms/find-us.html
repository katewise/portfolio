Where to find us in 2024

Kei kōnei mātou i te tau 2024:

Kohi-tātea - January

The annual EIGHTARMS fishing contest where women have their own category. Prizes range from a day out with Matt Watson on the ITM Fishing Show to Eightarms merch. Sign up to our emails to receive correspondence about how to enter.

Poutū-te-rangi - March

Got half an hour to spare on a Sunday night? Tune into channel three to see us on Matt Watson’s ITM Fishing Show. This episode will host the winner of our fishing competition and have a special place for your Mrs on the boat.

Check us out at our next event for some free stuff!

Paenga-whāwhā - April

Another fishing competition? We say yes. This is where all you blokes flock to and therefore - so will we. Come say hi when we do our rounds and grab yourself some sick merch and buckyu hats galore.

Haratua -May

Are your shirts covered in holes? Get a new one at the Hutchwilco Boat Show at the Auckland Viaduct on the 14th March. We will have a large range of t-shirts, hoodies, hats and bags. Everything you need for a day out on the water.

Bring your Mrs to the 90 mile beach fishing comp this year and win some eightarms merch!

Do you have a story about inequality you would like to share? Send it to us hello@eightarms.co.nz we would love to get in touch!
